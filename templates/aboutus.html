Our Journey!

ICE TRACK, formally known as Tom and Jerry's Ice Cream Code, is a software created to assist Tom and Adam's Ice Cream Company in selling their famous ice cream. The software aims to be able take your orders of their popular ice cream flavors and ship your orders out in a timely manner so that you can the best ice cream! Serving the San Diego community for over 500 years, we want to continue serving the best community in California. With a wide variety of flavors to choose from, ranging from Strawberry to Chocolate, to our classic Vanilla, you'll never run out of the everexpanding flavors to choose from! If you have any questions, feel free to go to our trouble shooting system to submit a ticket and we'll get back to you right away!
